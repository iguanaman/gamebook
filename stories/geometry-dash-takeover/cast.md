# Geometry Dash: The Takeover — Cast

Order: first appearance in the gauntlet (foyer → room 7). Narrator is set in `story.yaml` and is not listed here. All kids are 10 years old. All kid dialogue (and narration, since the player is a kid) is written in 10-year-old voice — short sentences, slang, exclamations, ALL CAPS for hype, gamer/GD slang. RobTop is the only adult — quirky inventor showman energy, still kid-friendly.

---

## RobTop (eccentric host, retiring studio founder, the only adult)
**Voice:** `male_young_american_forthright` — forthright, declamatory, showman-like.
**Body:** cyan velvet blazer with stitched-on cube icons; oversized neon-rim glasses he keeps pushing up his nose; finger always tapping a rhythm.
**Voice on the page:** declamatory, riddling, rhetorical questions, three-part lists ("you will hop, you will flip, you will — possibly — be flung"). Calls the kids "my finalists" / "young geometricians." Never raises his voice. Refuses the word "lose" — prefers "step aside," "rejoin the audience," "take an early bow."
**Contradiction:** giddy showman hiding the cold fact that he's auditioning a successor by attrition; performance of fairness matters more than the kids do.
**First seen:** `foyer`. Recurs in every elimination (PA), `room5_wave_chamber` (in person — 1v1 final), `win_heir`.

## Mara (rival kid, out Room 1)
**Voice:** `female_young_american_youthful_sweet`.
**Body:** oversized creator-points hoodie pulled past her knuckles; pink high-tops scuffed grey; chews the hoodie drawstring.

**Contradiction:** loudest kid, knows the least.
**Out:** `room1_icon_foundry` — shouts triangle, dunked through trapdoor.

## Brennan (rival kid, out Room 2)
**Voice:** `male_young_american_youthful_confident_bright`.
**Body:** sneakers untied, laces flopping; cracked tablet under one arm; flicks the tablet like it'll whisper answers.
**Voice on the page:** "Bro—," "Easy—," "I literally—." Quotes his own jump counts. Confuses mode names with total certainty.
**Contradiction:** props of mastery without the patience.
**Out:** `room2_mode_hangar` — shouts wrong mode, scooped sideways by ship-mode wind tunnel.

## Milo (rival kid, out Room 3) — jittery savant
**Voice:** `male_young_american_weak` — thin, fragile timbre fits a kid who knows the trivia cold but loses his nerve at speed.
**Body:** small for his age, swallowed by an oversized GD hoodie; chewed hoodie strings; rocks heel-to-toe nonstop; hands flutter when he talks.
**Voice on the page:** rapid-fire then trails off — "It's the — wait — it's the — yellow? no — green—." Mutters icon names under his breath like a calming chant. Apologises before answering ("sorry, sorry — I think—").
**Contradiction:** the most genuinely knowledgeable rival except Dex; eliminated by panic, not ignorance.
**Out:** `room3_portal_workshop` — blurts wrong portal colour, gravity flips, drifts ceiling-ward into OUT chute.

## Sage (rival kid, out Room 4)
**Voice:** `female_young_american_sweet_youthful_clear`.
**Body:** dark braid pulled tight; single black wristband; weight back on her heels, arms folded.
**Voice on the page:** short sentences, no filler. Two or three words when she answers at all. Watches RobTop's face.
**Contradiction:** seems like the threat — but overthinks pad physics.
**Out:** `room4_jump_pad_assembly` — picks pink instead of red, fired in a long neon arc into bleachers.

## Dex (main rival, out Room 5 — 1v1 final)
**Voice:** `male_young_american_youthful_friendly_modern` — smug-but-prepared, the kid who has actually done the homework and wants you to know.
**Body:** GD-cube enamel pin on a clean black tee; hair gelled to a perfect spike; closed-mouth smile when someone else is wrong.
**Voice on the page:** still a 10yo — but a smug one. "Actually," "technically," "to be fair." Singles out the player as the real competition. Drops creator names like flexes. Still uses kid slang sometimes ("bruh, easy") when he's feeling himself.
**Contradiction:** best-prepared kid is also the most certain — that certainty gets him on Chamber of Time lore.
**Out:** `room5_wave_chamber` — confidently says wrong answer to the Chamber of Time question; sluiced down the wave tube as the player wins. Eliminated in the opening beat of `win_heir`.
